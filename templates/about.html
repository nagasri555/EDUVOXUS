About EduVoxus

Transforming traditional learning into smart, interactive experiences

EduVoxus is an AI-powered learning platform designed to make studying more interactive, engaging and effective. Students learn through quizzes, descriptive questions, voice-based practice, AI doubt solving, discussion forums, and structured courses - all with real-time performance tracking and gamification.

Platform Features

Everything you need for smart, effective learning

AI Question Generation
Dynamically generates questions on any topic using GPT-4. No pre-loaded content needed.
Interactive Quiz Mode
MCQ quizzes with instant scoring, answer review, and performance tracking.
Voice-Based Practice
Practice speaking with browser speech recognition and AI evaluation of answers.
AI Tutor Chatbot
Ask any doubt and get instant, clear AI-powered explanations and solutions.
Gamification & Leaderboard
Earn points, badges, maintain streaks and compete on the leaderboard.
Discussion Forum
Ask questions, share knowledge, and get AI-powered answers in the community.
Course Management
Structured courses with study material uploads, managed by teachers and admins.
Certificate Generation
Earn printable certificates for high scores to showcase your achievements.
Adaptive Analytics
Track performance, identify weak topics, and get personalized recommendations.
